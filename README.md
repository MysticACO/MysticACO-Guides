## **How This Works**

Mystic ACO uses automated checkout systems to attempt purchases on your behalf during product releases. Instead of manually trying to secure items yourself, you submit your checkout information ahead of time and our system handles the checkout attempt when the item goes live.

Once you submit your entry, your profile is prepared and queued for the release. When the drop begins, our bots attempt to complete the checkout using the information you provided, including your account login, payment method, shipping address, and IMAP email connection if verification is required.

Because many retailers use anti-bot protections and limited inventory systems, success is never guaranteed. However, using automated checkout significantly increases the chances of securing items compared to manual checkout attempts.

If your entry is successful, the order will be placed using your provided payment method and shipping details. You will then receive confirmation and have **72 hours to complete the Pay After Success (PAS) payment** for the service.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**If your entry is unsuccessful, no payment is required**
{% endhint %}

***

## **Overview**

Mystic ACO supports a variety of releases across multiple retailers. Our system attempts checkouts using your submitted profile information during product drops.

**Supported retailers may include:**

* Walmart
* Target
* Pokemon Center
* Shopify Sites
* Nike / SNKRS
* Other Limited Releases

{% hint style="info" %}
Release availability will always be posted in the **ACO section of the server** before each drop.
{% endhint %}
