## What Is Jigging?

Jigging is one of the most important techniques when aiming to secure multiple orders. It involves slightly modifying your billing or shipping details so they appear unique to retailers, while still ensuring your packages arrive at the correct destination. ## Common Jigging Methods

Retailers often enforce strict quantity limits and will cancel duplicate orders tied to the same address or account. By applying effective jigging methods, you can increase your chances of bypassing these restrictions, securing multiple items, and reducing the risk of order cancellations. 

**1. Address Line 2 Jig**

If your shipping address is not tied to an actual apartment or unit, you can **"fake"** one by adding a unit number at the end of your address or in **Address Line 2.**

> **After:** 1234 Mystic Drive, Apt 1
