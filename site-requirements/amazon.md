# Amazon

## Requirements

* Amazon account (Prime not required)
* A default Payment Method / Address is set for the account

## Amazon 2FA Security Key

* Allows us to log in without needing you to provide us 2FA codes

### Step 5: Copy and **SAVE** this key to your files

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://help.refractbot.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2Fjdmk3RduT9OdU2B3zFmG%2Fblobs%2FhUp3aFB8O1kfews1qvIk%2Fimage.png&#x26;width=300&#x26;dpr=3&#x26;quality=100&#x26;sign=b3219bf4&#x26;sv=2" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* From there, scan the QR code in your favorite MFA App
* Once your key is **saved**, and you have scanned the QR code, **insert the code** from the app and click "Verify OTP and continue"
