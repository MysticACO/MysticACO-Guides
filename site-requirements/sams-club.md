# Sams Club

## Requirements

* A Sam's Club membership is required in order to be eligible for ACO. [Purchase Here](https://www.samsclub.com/join)
* Sam's Club Accounts are required **(Account Username + Account Password)**
* Each submission must have unique addresses or Jigged variations [Click Here](../how-to-jig.md)
* IMAP is **required** in order for us to grab your 2FA codes. [Click Here](../imap.md)
